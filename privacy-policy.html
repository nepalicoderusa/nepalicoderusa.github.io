Privacy Policy

Holy Bible KJV — King James Version

Effective Date: April 6, 2025
We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. This policy explains how the Holy Bible KJV app handles your data.
· · · ✦ · · ·

1. No Personal Data Collected

The Holy Bible KJV app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information — such as your name, email address, location, or any other identifying data. There are no user accounts and no registration is required to use the app.

All app data — including bookmarks, highlights, reading progress, and preferences — is stored locally on your device only and never leaves it.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob to support free access to the app. AdMob may automatically collect certain information to serve ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address and general location
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed solely by Google. We do not receive or store any of this information. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for full details on how AdMob handles your data.

3. Your Ad Choices

You can opt out of personalized ads at any time through your Android device settings:

  • Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

4. Children's Privacy

The Holy Bible KJV app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by an updated effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
